Hi, I'm Madeline!

I'm a videographer and here's a little about me!

To start from the very beginning, I was born in 2004 in Whittier, California, and was adopted at birth. Since then, I've been proud to call the East Bay my home. I learned I found the most comfort in playing sports, doing crafts, and spending time with friends and family (all things in which I still find comfort today). After those first exciting chapters of my life, I ventured back to Southern California to study at UCLA where I earned a BA in Philosophy with minors in Global Studies and Film, Television, & Digital Media. I found my "home away from home" at the Daily Bruin, the school's student-run news publication. I'm now back in the Bay and looking for more opportunities to learn and contribute to meaningful projects!

(I obviously can't tell you all "about me" in one "about me" section, so if you're interested in hearing more, please let me know because I'd love to hear about you, too! Also let me know if you want to talk about films or modern philosophy!)
